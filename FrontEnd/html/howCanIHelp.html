How Can I Help the planet?

The small changes of your life can do more than you think...

Check the changes that you can make!

Did you know that shower shoud durate 5 minutes, wasting only 100L of water? That means that for every minute that you waste in the shower, you're wasting 20L of water

If we brush our teeth with the tap constantly open, we can spend an average of 20 liters in each wash, which is an average of at least 60 liters of water (if we brush our teeth three times a day)

If we take a bathtub, we wasted almost 300L, instead of the 100L that you waste with a 5 minute shower

There's more things that we can make to help the planet!

  • Make sure the washer is completely full before using it.

  • Make sure the Dishwasher is completely full before using it.

  • Low-flush toilets use less water per flush than older models. Consider updating them if possible.
