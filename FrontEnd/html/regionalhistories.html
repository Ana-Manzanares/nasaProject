Changing Waters

Impact of Climate Change in Four Regions of the World

Welcome to a journey around the world where we'll explore a highly relevant topic: climate change and its impact on the global water cycle. Climate change is a global phenomenon that is altering the way our natural environment functions, and one of its most evident effects lies in the distribution and availability of the most essential resource for life: water. As we progress through this story, we'll focus on four particular regions, each with its own unique tale of challenges and adaptation in the face of a changing climate. These regions, though geographically distant, share a vital connection through the water cycle, which is influenced by climate transformations on a global scale.
